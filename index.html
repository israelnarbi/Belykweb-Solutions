Let's Chart

Quarme Israel is a dynamic and talented young man based in Accra, currently in his final year as a Computer Science student at Accra Technical University. With a passion for creativity and technology, he has established himself as a proficient graphic designer and web developer. His skills extend into the digital marketing realm, where he expertly manages Google Business Profiles and creates compelling online presences for various businesses.

In addition to his tech-savvy skills, Quarme is a vlogger who shares insights and knowledge about the industry, making complex concepts accessible to a broader audience. He offers general printing and branding services, helping clients create memorable visual identities. Driven by a desire to empower others, he conducts master classes in IT, sharing his expertise and inspiring the next generation of tech enthusiasts. With a blend of creativity, technical skills, and a commitment to education, Quarme Israel is poised to make a significant impact in the digital landscape
